Who we are

Pick n Pay is Israel’s leading retailer. The Company aims to provide high-quality and diverse solutions to all its customers.

In 1958, Pick n Pay opened its first store in Tel Aviv - the first modern supermarket of its kind in Israel. The success of that first store was leveraged into growth, resulting in Pick n Pay’s current position:

  • Hundreds of branches across Israel - from Kiryat Shmona in the north to Eilat in the south;
  • An average of 2 million weekly customers and 1.8 million customer club members;  
  • The Pick n Pay private label, which offers thousands of high-quality products at reduced prices;
